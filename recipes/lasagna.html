Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Ricotta cheese
  • Spices
  • Pasta sauce
  • Beef and sausage
  • Garlic
  • Egg
  • Mozzarella
  • Parsley
  • Parmesan
  • Tomato paste
  • Onion
  1. Boil pasta: Cook in a large pot of salted water per the recipe below
  2. Prepare meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce and simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Combine cheese mixture: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. Layer and bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until browned and bubbly
