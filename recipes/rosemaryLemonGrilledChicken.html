Rosemary Lemon Grilled Chicken

Description

Rosemary-lemon grilled chicken features chicken, often breasts, marinated or brushed with a flavorful combination of lemon juice, zest, fresh rosemary, garlic, and olive oil, then grilled until tender and juicy.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup fresh rosemary
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 lemon, zested
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 6 (6 ounce) skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • salt and pepper to taste
